R-Ladies San Diego

R-Ladies San Diego is a group with diverse skill levels, ranging from 8 beginners to 3 women how have taught R classes or tutorials.

ggplot(data_sd, aes(x = level)) +
  geom_bar() +

Regarding skill interests, San Diegan R-Ladies are particularly interested in networking, other R aspects, and packages.
